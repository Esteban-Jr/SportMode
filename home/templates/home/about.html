About SportsMode

Our passion for sport drives everything we do.

Our Mission

SportsMode was founded with one goal in mind: to make premium sports equipment accessible to every athlete, from beginners to professionals. We believe the right gear shouldn't hold anyone back.

Whether you're kicking off your first season of football, joining a local basketball league, picking up a tennis racket for the first time, or building a home gym — we have the equipment you need to hit the ground running.

Our Story

SportsMode started as a small side project built by a group of sports enthusiasts who couldn't find a single reliable online destination for multi-sport equipment in the UK. Every store seemed to specialise in just one sport, or offer poor-quality products at inflated prices.

So we built one ourselves. Starting with a curated selection across four sports — football, basketball, tennis, and fitness — we've grown into a trusted destination for thousands of customers across the UK, all united by a love of sport.

2020

SportsMode founded with a vision for multi-sport retail done right.

2021

Launched with 100+ products across football and fitness.

2022

Expanded into basketball and tennis. Hit 5,000 customers.

Today

500+ products, 10k+ customers, and still growing.
