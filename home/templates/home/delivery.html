Returns Policy

We want you to be completely happy with your purchase. If something isn't right, you can return it to us within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Conditions for Returns

  • Items must be unused and in their original packaging.
  • Tags and labels must still be attached.
  • Personalised or custom items cannot be returned unless faulty.
  • Proof of purchase (your order confirmation email) is required.

How to Return

  1. Log in to your account and visit your Order History.
  2. Select the item(s) you wish to return and submit a return request.
  3. Print the prepaid returns label we send to your email.
  4. Drop your parcel at any Post Office or collection point.
  5. Your refund will be processed within 3–5 working days of us receiving the return.
Faulty or incorrect items? If you receive a damaged or wrong item, please contact us and we'll resolve it immediately at no cost to you.
